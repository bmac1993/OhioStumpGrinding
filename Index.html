Our Services

We do full removal for replants and grinding below grade for areas that will be covered with grass or mulch. We offer everything from "grind and go" to full service cleanup.

We specialize in carefully removing landscape bushes, roots, ornamental trees and stumps without damaging existing landscape. Landscape designers often chose us for removal and prep for new landscape.

Reclaim your land! From pesky weeds in your yard to overgrown areas with brush and saplings-we take it down and haul it out. Brush is cut and stumps and roots are removed.

All firewood is a mixture of hardwoods that is tested with a moisture meter for readiness to burn. Prices include FREE delivery and stacking within 20 miles of Ashville, Ohio (except for dumped cord).

Storm damage, natural decay, and many other factors can cause fallen branches, trees, and other debris. We offer complete debris removal, hauling, and clean up. Prices vary based on size and location.
